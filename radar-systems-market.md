<p><strong>The Radar Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulsed Radar</li><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Radar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radar systems market is categorized into Pulsed Radar and Continuous Wave (CW) Radar. Pulsed Radar transmits short bursts of radio waves, allowing for precise distance measurement and object detection,making it ideal for applications like air traffic control and weather monitoring. Continuous Wave Radar, on the other hand, emits a continuous signal, providing real-time velocity measurements, making it suitable for traffic enforcement and collision avoidance systems. Each type serves distinct applications, influencing market demand and development.</p></p>
