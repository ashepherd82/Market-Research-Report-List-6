<p><p>The SUS-Al-alloy clad metals market is categorized into bilayer and three-layer structures, each serving distinct applications. Bilayer structures consist of a single layer of aluminum alloy bonded to stainless steel, offering excellent corrosion resistance and strength. In contrast, three-layer structures comprise an additional aluminum layer or interlayer that enhances thermal and electrical conductivity. These configurations are widely used in industries requiring lightweight yet sturdy materials, benefiting sectors like aerospace, automotive, and construction for improved performance and durability.</p></p>
