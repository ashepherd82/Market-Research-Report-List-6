<p><strong>The Chemical Anchors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Injectable Adhensive Anchors</li><li>Capsule Adhensive Anchors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chemical anchors market comprises various types that facilitate strong bonding in construction and engineering. Injectable adhesive anchors involve a two-component system where adhesive is mixed and injected into drilled holes, providing flexibility in curing and load capacity. Capsule adhesive anchors come pre-packaged with adhesive in capsules, which break upon insertion into the hole, releasing the adhesive for use. Both types ensure reliable anchoring in concrete, masonry, and similar materials, catering to diverse applications in structural integrity and safety.</p></p>
