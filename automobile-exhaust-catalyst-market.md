<p><strong>The Automobile Exhaust Catalyst Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Three-way Catalyst (TWC)</li><li>Four-Way Catalyst (4WC)</li><li>Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC)</li><li>Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) Catalyst</li><li>Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Catalyst</li><li>Ammonia Slip Catalyst (ASC)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile exhaust catalyst market comprises various types designed to reduce harmful emissions. The Three-Way Catalyst (TWC) simultaneously converts carbon monoxide, hydrocarbons, and nitrogen oxides. The Four-Way Catalyst (4WC) adds the ability to reduce particulate matter. The Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC) facilitates the oxidation of diesel emissions, while the Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) Catalyst uses urea to convert nitrogen oxides into harmless nitrogen and water. Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Catalysts trap soot, and Ammonia Slip Catalyst (ASC) reduces unreacted ammonia in SCR systems. Other catalysts address specific or emerging needs within the market.</p></p>
