<p><ul><li>AMR (Anisotropic Magnetoresistance Effect)</li><li>GMR (Giant Magnetoresistance Effect)</li><li>TMR (Tunnel Magnetoresistance Effect)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive magnetic sensor market encompasses various technologies, primarily including Anisotropic Magnetoresistance (AMR), Giant Magnetoresistance (GMR), and Tunnel Magnetoresistance (TMR). AMR sensors detect magnetic fields by exploiting changes in resistance based on magnetization direction. GMR sensors offer enhanced sensitivity and are used in applications like wheel speed sensing. TMR sensors provide even greater sensitivity due to quantum tunneling effects and are ideal for advanced automotive systems. These technologies play crucial roles in improving vehicle safety and performance.</p></p>
