<p><p>The standard tactile switch market is characterized by two main types of mounting: surface-mounting and through-hole mounting. Surface-mounting switches are designed for direct attachment onto the surface of printed circuit boards (PCBs), offering a compact design and efficient use of space. In contrast, through-hole mounting switches feature leads that pass through holes in the PCB, providing enhanced durability and stability, making them suitable for applications requiring robust connections. Each type serves distinct applications and design requirements in electronics.</p></p>
