<p><p>The Sol-Gel Processing Market encompasses various coating techniques, including rotational coating, immersion coating, roll coating, and others. Rotational coating involves rotating a substrate to achieve a uniform layer of material. Immersion coating submerges the substrate in a sol-gel solution for even coverage. Roll coating applies a thin film through contact with rotating rollers, ensuring consistent thickness. Other methods may include spraying or dipping techniques. Each approach offers unique advantages in terms of efficiency, material usage, and application versatility.</p></p>
