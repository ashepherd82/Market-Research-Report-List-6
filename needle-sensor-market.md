<p><p>The needle sensor market is segmented into two main types: contactless sensors and piercing sensors. Contactless sensors utilize non-invasive technology to detect and monitor physiological parameters without the need for direct skin penetration, making them ideal for continuous health monitoring. In contrast, piercing sensors involve the insertion of a needle into the skin to collect blood or other fluids for analysis, offering precise measurements and immediate results. Both types cater to different applications in medical diagnostics and wearables.</p></p>
