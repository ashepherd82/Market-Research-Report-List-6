<p><p>The titanium-based alloys market is classified into three main types: Alpha, Beta, and Alpha + Beta alloys. Alpha titanium alloys are known for their excellent high-temperature strength and corrosion resistance, making them suitable for aerospace applications. Beta titanium alloys, on the other hand, offer superior formability and weldability, ideal for biomedical and industrial uses. Alpha + Beta alloys combine the desirable properties of both types, providing a balanced performance in various applications, including automotive and aerospace industries.</p></p>
