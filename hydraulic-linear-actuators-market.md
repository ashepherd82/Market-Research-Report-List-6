<p><strong>The Hydraulic Linear Actuators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Acting</li><li>Double-Acting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic linear actuator market is primarily divided into two types: single-acting and double-acting actuators. Single-acting actuators utilize hydraulic pressure for movement in one direction, relying on springs or external forces for return, making them simpler and cost-effective for applications like lifting. Conversely, double-acting actuators use hydraulic pressure to control movement in both directions, offering greater versatility and power for more complex tasks, commonly found in machine operation and automation applications. Each type serves distinct user needs based on functionality and efficiency.</p></p>
