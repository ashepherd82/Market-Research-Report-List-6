<p><ul><li>Hard Tag</li><li>Soft Tag</li><li>Deactivator or Detacher</li><li>Detection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EAS (Electronic Article Surveillance) systems encompass several market types, including hard tags, soft tags, deactivators, and detection systems. Hard tags are robust security devices attached to merchandise, designed to deter theft through alarms. Soft tags are lightweight, often paper-based, and used for less valuable items. Deactivators disable tags at the point of sale, allowing legitimate purchases to be made without triggering alarms. Detection systems, placed at exits, sense active tags to alert staff of potential theft, ensuring effective loss prevention.</p></p>
