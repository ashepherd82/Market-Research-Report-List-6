<p><ul><li>External Drum Laser Photoplotters</li><li>Internal Drum Laser Photoplotters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB photoplotters market is primarily divided into two main types: external drum and internal drum laser photoplotters. External drum laser photoplotters utilize an external rotating drum to hold the photomask material, providing high precision and larger formats, making them suitable for complex designs. In contrast, internal drum laser photoplotters house the photomask material internally, offering compact designs and quicker processing times. Both types cater to the growing demand for high-quality printed circuit boards in various electronic applications.</p></p>
