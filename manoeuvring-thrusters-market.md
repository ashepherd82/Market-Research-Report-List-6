<p><p>Manoeuvring thrusters are essential for enhancing vessel control and maneuverability. Tunnel thrusters, installed in tunnels beneath the hull, provide lateral thrust to aid in docking and navigating tight spaces. Waterjet bow thrusters utilize high-velocity water jets for agile steering and quick responses. Both types improve a ship's handling in various conditions, with tunnel thrusters offering stability and waterjet thrusters delivering rapid directional changes. Together, they play a pivotal role in modern maritime operations.</p></p>
