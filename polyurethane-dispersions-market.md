<p><p>The polyurethane dispersions market is primarily categorized into water-based and solvent-based systems. Water-based polyurethane dispersions are eco-friendly, offering low VOC emissions and are suitable for various applications such as coatings, adhesives, and inks. They provide excellent flexibility and durability. Solvent-based polyurethane dispersions, while effective for high-performance applications, contain higher VOC levels and may be less environmentally friendly. These dispersions are often used in industries requiring strong adhesion and chemical resistance, such as automotive and industrial coatings.</p></p>
