<p><ul><li>Bar Chip</li><li>Single Tube Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-power semiconductor laser chip market is primarily categorized into two types: bar chips and single tube chips. Bar chips consist of multiple laser diodes arranged in a linear fashion, offering higher output powers and efficiency, making them ideal for applications like industrial laser systems. Single tube chips, on the other hand, contain a single laser diode within a compact package, providing a focused beam for applications like telecommunications and medical devices. Both types serve distinct market needs based on performance and application requirements.</p></p>
