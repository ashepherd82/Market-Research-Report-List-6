<p><ul><li>BiCMOS</li><li>RFCMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radar transceivers are essential components in advanced radar systems, and they can be categorized mainly into two types: BiCMOS and RF-CMOS. BiCMOS utilizes both bipolar and CMOS transistors, offering high performance, low noise, and improved linearity, suitable for applications requiring precision. On the other hand, RF-CMOS leverages standard CMOS technology, providing cost-effectiveness and integration capabilities. This makes it ideal for high-volume consumer products. Both types serve distinct market needs, influencing their adoption across various sectors, including automotive and defense.</p></p>
