<p><p>The paint marker market is primarily divided into two types: liquid type and solid type. Liquid type markers contain ink that flows smoothly, offering vibrant colors and precise applications for various surfaces. They are ideal for detailed work and can easily be refilled. In contrast, solid type markers, often resembling crayons or chalk, provide a durable, mess-free option with a thicker application. These markers are excellent for bold lines and are commonly used in industrial settings and for outdoor projects.</p></p>
