<p><ul><li>Single</li><li>Double</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air pilot valves are control devices used in pneumatic systems to regulate airflow. The market is segmented into single and double types. Single air pilot valves have one control port, allowing airflow in one direction, making them simpler and cost-effective for basic applications. Double air pilot valves feature two control ports, enabling bidirectional airflow control, offering greater flexibility and precision for complex systems. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, balancing simplicity and advanced functionality.</p></p>
