<p><p>The High-Performance KVM Switches market is categorized into two main types: Analog High-Performance KVM Switches and Digital High-Performance KVM Switches. Analog switches utilize traditional signal transmission methods, allowing users to control multiple systems over analog connections, typically offering lower performance in terms of resolution and distance. In contrast, Digital High-Performance KVM Switches leverage digital technology, supporting higher resolutions, longer distances, and enhanced features like USB and audio support, making them ideal for data centers and environments requiring superior performance.</p></p>
