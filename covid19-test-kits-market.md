<p><p>The COVID-19 test kit market consists primarily of two types: nucleic acid test kits and antibody test kits. Nucleic acid test kits, such as PCR tests, detect the virus's genetic material, providing accurate results for active infections. In contrast, antibody test kits identify antibodies produced in response to the virus, indicating past infections. Each type serves distinct purposes, with nucleic acid tests primarily used for diagnosis during active illness, while antibody tests help assess immunity and population exposure.</p></p>
