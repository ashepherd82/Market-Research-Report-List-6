<p><strong>The Wearable Cardiac Monitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric Pulse Based</li><li>Optical Technology Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wearable cardiac monitors are categorized into two primary types: electric pulse-based and optical technology-based devices. Electric pulse-based monitors utilize electrodes to detect heart activity through electrical signals, providing accurate data on heart rhythms and abnormalities. In contrast, optical technology-based monitors employ light sensors to measure blood flow and heart rate by analyzing changes in light absorption. Both types serve crucial roles in continuous heart monitoring, enabling early detection of cardiovascular issues and supporting personalized healthcare.</p></p>
