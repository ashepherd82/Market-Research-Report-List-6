<p><ul><li>Bullet Mufflers</li><li>Chambered Mufflers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car muffler market includes various types designed to reduce exhaust noise and improve performance. **Bullet mufflers** are straight-through designs with perforated tubes and sound-absorbing materials, offering minimal restriction and a sporty tone. **Chambered mufflers** use internal chambers to reflect sound waves, reducing noise while maintaining a deep, aggressive exhaust note. Both types cater to different preferences, with bullet mufflers favoring performance and chambered mufflers balancing sound control and tone enhancement, appealing to car enthusiasts and everyday drivers alike.</p></p>
