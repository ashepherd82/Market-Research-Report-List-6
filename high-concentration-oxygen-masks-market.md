<p><p>The High Concentration Oxygen Masks market is primarily categorized into two types: Rebreathing and Non-Rebreathing high concentration oxygen masks. Rebreathing masks allow for a controlled mixture of exhaled and inhaled air, enabling patients to receive higher oxygen levels, useful in certain medical scenarios. In contrast, Non-Rebreathing masks provide nearly pure oxygen by preventing exhaled air from re-entering, making them ideal for critical care situations where rapid oxygen delivery is essential. Both serve vital roles in respiratory support and treatment.</p></p>
