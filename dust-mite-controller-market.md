<p><p>The Dust Mite Controller market is divided into two main types: Vertical Dust Mite Controllers and Horizontal Dust Mite Controllers. Vertical controllers are designed to operate in upright positions, typically targeting surfaces like mattresses and upholstery, using airflow to capture dust mites. In contrast, Horizontal Dust Mite Controllers function while lying flat, often covering larger areas such as carpets and floors.
