<p><p>The additives for coatings and inks market includes various specialized agents that enhance performance. Dispersing agents optimize pigment distribution, ensuring uniform color and consistency. Rheology control agents modify the flow and viscosity, improving application ease. Leveling agents promote a smooth surface finish by reducing surface tension, while anti-foam agents prevent bubble formation during application. Adhesion promoting agents enhance the bonding between layers, improving durability and adhesion on various substrates. Together, these additives play a crucial role in the quality and functionality of coatings and inks.</p></p>
