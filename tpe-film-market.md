<p><p>The TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer) film market is typically categorized into ventilated and non-ventilated types. Ventilated TPE films are designed with perforations or breathable features, allowing air and moisture to pass through, making them suitable for applications such as medical packaging and clothing. Non-ventilated TPE films, on the other hand, are solid and offer a barrier against environmental factors, often used in packaging and protective coverings where airtightness is crucial. Each type serves specific functional needs in various industries.</p></p>
