<p><p>Brushless Interior Permanent Magnet (IPM) motors are categorized into two main types: DC Brushless IPM Motors and AC Brushless IPM Motors. DC Brushless IPM Motors typically operate on direct current and utilize electronic commutation, offering high efficiency and precise control, making them ideal for applications like electric vehicles and robotics. In contrast, AC Brushless IPM Motors function on alternating current, providing higher torque and robustness, and are commonly used in industrial automation and HVAC systems, enhancing performance and energy efficiency.</p></p>
