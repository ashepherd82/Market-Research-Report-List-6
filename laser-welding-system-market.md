<p><p>The laser welding system market features two primary types: solid-state laser welding machines and CO2 laser welding machines. Solid-state laser welding machines use diodes or fiber optics to generate high-power laser beams, offering precision and efficiency for metal joining. In contrast, CO2 laser welding machines utilize a gas laser, providing versatility for various materials, especially thicker metals. Both technologies cater to diverse industrial applications, enhancing production quality and speed while minimizing thermal distortion and oxidation during the welding process.</p></p>
