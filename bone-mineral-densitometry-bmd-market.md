<p><p>Bone Mineral Densitometry (BMD) is a diagnostic technique used to measure bone density, helping to assess osteoporosis and fracture risk. The market is segmented into two main types: X-ray and ultrasonic methods. X-ray BMD, using dual-energy X-ray absorptiometry (DXA), is the gold standard, providing precise measurements but involves low radiation exposure. Ultrasonic BMD, using sound waves, offers a radiation-free alternative but may be less accurate. Both methods cater to distinct clinical needs in evaluating bone health.</p></p>
