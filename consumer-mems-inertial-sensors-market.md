<p><p>The Consumer MEMS Inertial Sensors Market consists of three primary types: accelerometers, gyroscopes, and magnetometers. Accelerometers measure linear acceleration and tilt, enabling applications such as screen orientation and fitness tracking. Gyroscopes measure rotational motion, enhancing navigation and stability in devices like smartphones and drones. Magnetometers detect magnetic fields, aiding in compass functionalities and location services. Together, these sensors enhance device performance and user experiences across various consumer electronics, including smartphones, wearables, and gaming systems.</p></p>
