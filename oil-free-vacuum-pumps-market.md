<p><p>The oil-free vacuum pumps market includes various types suited for different applications. Screw vacuum pumps utilize twin screws to create a vacuum, ensuring a continuous flow. Rotary vane vacuum pumps use rotating vanes in a chamber for efficient suction. Piston vacuum pumps employ a reciprocating piston to generate vacuum pressure. Membrane vacuum pumps utilize flexible membranes to create vacuum without lubrication.
