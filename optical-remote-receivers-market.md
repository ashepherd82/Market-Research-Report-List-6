<p><strong>The Optical Remote Receivers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Receiving Type</li><li>Passive Receiving Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical remote receivers market is categorized into two main types: active and passive receiving types. Active receiving types utilize powered components to amplify and process signals, ensuring improved performance over long distances. They often require additional power sources and are suitable for high-speed data transmission. Conversely, passive receiving types operate without external power, relying on natural light and minimal electronic components. These are generally simpler, cost-effective, and ideal for applications where low power consumption and basic signal processing are adequate.</p></p>
