<p><p>Veterinary ultrasound imaging market includes various types of imaging systems. 2D ultrasound imaging systems provide two-dimensional images of the internal structures of animals. Doppler ultrasound imaging systems are used to assess blood flow within the body. 3D or 4D ultrasound imaging systems offer three-dimensional or four-dimensional images, providing more detailed visuals of anatomical structures. Each type of imaging system caters to different diagnostic needs and plays a crucial role in veterinary medicine by allowing for non-invasive and accurate evaluation of animals' health.</p></p>
