<p><p>The kidney/renal cancer drugs market comprises three primary treatment types: targeted therapy, chemotherapy, and immunotherapy. Targeted therapies focus on specific molecular targets associated with cancer cells, enhancing efficacy while minimizing damage to healthy tissues. Chemotherapy uses cytotoxic drugs to kill rapidly dividing cancer cells but may affect normal cells, leading to various side effects. Immunotherapy harnesses the body's immune system to recognize and combat cancer cells, providing a potentially powerful and enduring response. Together, these approaches offer diverse treatment options.</p></p>
