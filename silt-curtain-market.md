<p><p>The Silt Curtain market is categorized into two main types: Woven Fibers and Non-Woven Fibers. Woven Fiber silt curtains are made from interlaced threads, providing enhanced strength and durability, making them suitable for high-flow applications. In contrast, Non-Woven Fiber silt curtains are created from bonded fibers, offering excellent filtration and water permeability, ideal for reducing sediment and contaminants in calm water environments. Both types are essential in construction, environmental protection, and water management projects.</p></p>
