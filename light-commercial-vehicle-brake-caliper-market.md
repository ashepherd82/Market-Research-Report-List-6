<p><strong>The Light Commercial Vehicle Brake Caliper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Piston Caliper</li><li>Multi-Piston Caliper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light commercial vehicle brake caliper market features two primary types: single piston calipers and multi-piston calipers. Single piston calipers utilize a single piston to push the brake pads against the rotor, offering a simple and cost-effective solution for lighter loads. In contrast, multi-piston calipers incorporate multiple pistons, enhancing braking power, stability, and heat dissipation, making them suitable for heavier loads and performance-oriented applications. Both types are essential for ensuring reliable braking performance in light commercial vehicles.</p></p>
