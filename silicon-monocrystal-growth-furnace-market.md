<p><strong>The Silicon Monocrystal Growth Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Czochralski (CZ) Method Furnace</li><li>Floating Zone (FZ) Method Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Silicon Monocrystal Growth Furnace market consists of two primary types: Czochralski (CZ) method furnaces and Floating Zone (FZ) method furnaces. CZ furnaces utilize a seed crystal dipped into molten silicon to grow a single crystal by pulling it upwards, making it ideal for large-scale production. In contrast, FZ furnaces achieve monocrystal growth through a melting zone created by moving heat, enabling the production of ultra-pure silicon crystals suitable for high-performance applications. Both methods cater to the semiconductor and photovoltaic industries.</p></p>
