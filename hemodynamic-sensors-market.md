<p><p>The hemodynamic sensors market comprises various types designed to monitor cardiovascular and pulmonary functions. Activity sensors track physical movements, while heart rate variability sensors assess variations in heartbeats, indicating stress levels and overall health. Minute ventilation sensors measure the volume of air inhaled and exhaled, crucial for respiratory monitoring. Intra-thoracic impedance sensors assess fluid levels in the thoracic cavity, and implantable hemodynamic monitors provide continuous cardiovascular data, enhancing patient management and treatment outcomes in critical care settings.</p></p>
