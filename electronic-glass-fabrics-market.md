<p><ul><li>Plain Weave</li><li>Leno Weave</li><li>Twill Weave</li><li>Satin Weave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic glass fabrics market is categorized into several weaves, each offering unique properties. Plain weave provides excellent strength and stability, making it suitable for general applications. Leno weave features crossed yarns, enhancing dimensional stability and reducing fraying, ideal for high-performance applications. Twill weave offers better drapability and flexibility, commonly used in complex shapes. Satin weave provides a smooth surface and is often used for applications requiring aesthetics and reduced weight. Each type serves specific electronic and industrial needs.</p></p>
