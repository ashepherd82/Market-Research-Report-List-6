<p><p>The arterial stents market comprises two primary types: coronary stents and peripheral stents. Coronary stents are designed for patients with coronary artery disease, helping to maintain open arteries after angioplasty in the heart. Peripheral stents, on the other hand, are utilized in the treatment of vascular diseases affecting the limbs and other peripheral arteries, improving blood flow. Both types support the management of vascular conditions, aiding recovery and reducing the risk of complications related to arterial blockages.</p></p>
