<p><p>The semiconductor strain gauge market comprises various types based on fabrication methods. Integrated semiconductor strain gauges are embedded into circuits, offering compact designs. Thin film semiconductor strain gauges utilize vapor deposition techniques for enhanced sensitivity and durability. Diffusion semiconductor strain gauges employ doping processes for improved performance in harsh environments. Epitaxial semiconductor strain gauges are formed through layer-by-layer growth, providing high precision and reliability. Each type caters to specific applications across industries like automotive, aerospace, and structural monitoring.</p></p>
