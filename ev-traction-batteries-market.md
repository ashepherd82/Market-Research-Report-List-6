<p><p>The EV traction battery market comprises various types, each serving distinct applications. Open lead acid batteries are affordable but have limited energy density and lifespan. Pure lead batteries offer better performance and durability, ideal for high-demand settings. Gel batteries provide enhanced safety through a gel electrolyte, reducing risk of spillage. Lithium-ion batteries dominate the market due to their high energy density, long cycle life, and lightweight characteristics, making them the preferred choice for modern electric vehicles.</p></p>
