<p><ul><li>Single Reduction Gear</li><li>Double Reduction Gear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gear reducer market features primarily two types: single reduction and double reduction gear systems. Single reduction gears utilize one gear stage to reduce speed and increase torque, making them suitable for applications where space is limited and moderate torque is required. In contrast, double reduction gears consist of two gear stages, allowing for greater torque multiplication and efficiency at higher load capacities. These systems are ideal for heavy-duty applications, providing enhanced performance in various industrial settings.</p></p>
