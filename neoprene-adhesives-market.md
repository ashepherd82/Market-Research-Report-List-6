<p><p>The neoprene adhesives market comprises three main types: solvent type, emulsion type, and solvent-free liquid type. Solvent type adhesives contain organic solvents for effective bonding and quick drying but may emit volatile organic compounds. Emulsion type adhesives, which are water-based, offer low toxicity and easy application, making them suitable for various surfaces. Solvent-free liquid adhesives provide a more environmentally friendly option, featuring high performance with minimal environmental impact. Each type caters to different industrial and consumer needs based on application requirements.</p></p>
