<p><p>The wheat seed coating agent market comprises various types, including suspended agents, emulsions, and wettable powders. Suspended agents are formulated as liquid suspensions, providing a uniform coating that enhances seed performance. Emulsions combine oil and water-based ingredients for improved adhesion and efficacy. Wettable powders are solid formulations that disperse in water, allowing for easy application and efficient coverage.
