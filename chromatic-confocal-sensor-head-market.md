<p><strong>The Chromatic Confocal Sensor Head Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Displacement Sensor Head</li><li>Thickness Sensor Head</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chromatic confocal sensor head market comprises various types, including displacement sensor heads and thickness sensor heads. Displacement sensor heads are designed to measure the position of surfaces with high precision by analyzing light wavelengths reflected from surfaces. Conversely, thickness sensor heads evaluate material thickness by measuring the distance between the surface layers, providing critical data for quality control in manufacturing. Both types leverage chromatic confocal technology to enable non-contact, accurate measurements across diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
