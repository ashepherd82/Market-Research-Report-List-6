<p><p>The absorption tower market consists of three primary types: gas absorption towers, liquid absorption towers, and combined gas-liquid absorption towers. Gas absorption towers focus on removing contaminants from gas streams using liquid absorbents, often employed in industrial processes. Liquid absorption towers facilitate the absorption of gaseous substances into liquids, typically found in chemical manufacturing. Gas and liquid absorption towers integrate both methods, allowing for simultaneous treatment of gases and liquids, enhancing efficiency in various environmental and industrial applications.</p></p>
