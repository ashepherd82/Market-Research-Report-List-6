<p><strong>The Bulk Explosives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ammonium Nitrate Explosives (Powder)</li><li>ANFO</li><li>Emulsion Explosive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bulk explosives market is primarily categorized into three types: Ammonium Nitrate Explosives (ANE), ANFO (Ammonium Nitrate Fuel Oil), and Emulsion Explosives. Ammonium Nitrate Explosives consist of a blend of ammonium nitrate and other chemicals, providing high detonation velocity. ANFO combines ammonium nitrate with fuel oil, offering a cost-effective option for blasting in mining and construction. Emulsion Explosives are water-resistant, consisting of ammonium nitrate suspended in an oil-based emulsion, making them suitable for wet environments and enhanced performance in challenging conditions.</p></p>
