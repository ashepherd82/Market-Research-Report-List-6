<p><p>The Oily Water Separator (OWS) market encompasses three key technologies: mechanical, chemical, and membrane-based systems. Mechanical OWSs use gravity and coalescence to separate oil from water, making them simple and effective for lighter oil concentrations. Chemical OWSs employ chemical additives to enhance separation efficiency, targeting oily mixtures in industrial waste. Membrane-based OWSs utilize selective permeability to separate oil at a molecular level, offering high purification capabilities. Each type serves specific needs and complies with regulatory standards for environmental protection.</p></p>
