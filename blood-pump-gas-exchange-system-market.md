<p><p>The blood pump gas exchange system market includes single roller pump systems and double roller pump systems. Single roller pump systems utilize a single rotating roller to push blood through the system, while double roller pump systems use two rollers rotating in opposite directions for improved stability and flow control. Both systems are used in extracorporeal circulation processes such as cardiopulmonary bypass and hemodialysis, providing efficient gas exchange and blood circulation for patients undergoing medical procedures.</p></p>
