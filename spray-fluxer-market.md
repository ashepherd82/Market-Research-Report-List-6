<p><p>The Spray Fluxer market comprises three primary types: stationary, reciprocating, and standalone systems. Stationary fluxers are fixed installations that continuously apply flux in industrial processes. Reciprocating fluxers feature movable components, allowing for varied application across different surfaces and enhancing versatility. Standalone fluxers are independent units designed for specific tasks, providing flexibility and ease of use in smaller operations. Each type serves different manufacturing needs, optimizing flux application in processes like soldering and surface treatment.</p></p>
