<p><p>The electroactive polymer market encompasses various types, including conductive plastics, inherently conductive polymers (ICPs), and inherently dissipative polymers (IDPs). Conductive plastics are standard polymers infused with conductive fillers, enhancing their electrical properties. ICPs possess intrinsic conductivity without requiring additives, making them suitable for various applications. IDPs exhibit controlled electrical conductivity, allowing for static dissipation while maintaining their insulating properties. Together, these materials are vital for advancements in electronics, sensors, actuators, and anti-static applications across diverse industries.</p></p>
