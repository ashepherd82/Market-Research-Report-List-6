<p><strong>The Powder Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Soluble Powder Adhesive</li><li>Hot-Melt Powder Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The powder adhesive market includes two main types: soluble powder adhesive and hot-melt powder adhesive. Soluble powder adhesives are designed to dissolve in water, forming a strong bond ideal for paper, textiles, and packaging applications. In contrast, hot-melt powder adhesives are thermoplastic materials that melt when heated and solidify upon cooling, offering rapid bonding and versatility for various substrates, including wood, plastics, and metals. Both types cater to different industrial needs, enhancing production efficiency and product performance.</p></p>
