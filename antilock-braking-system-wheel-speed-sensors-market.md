<p><p>The Antilock Braking System (ABS) wheel speed sensors are critical components ensuring vehicle safety by preventing wheel lock-up during braking. There are three main types: </p><p>1. **Linear Wheel Speed Sensors** use direct measurements of the wheel's linear velocity, typically through a magnet and coil arrangement.</p><p>2. **Ring Wheel Speed Sensors** employ toothed rings to create pulse signals that represent wheel speed.</p><p>3. **Hall Type Wheel Speed Sensors** utilize Hall effect technology to detect changes in magnetic fields, providing precise speed readings for optimal braking performance.</p></p>
