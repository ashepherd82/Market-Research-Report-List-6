<p><p>The agarose market is categorized into several types, including standard melting agarose and low melting point agarose. Standard melting agarose is primarily used for gel electrophoresis and DNA analysis, offering high resolution for nucleic acid separation. Low melting point agarose, on the other hand, is utilized for applications requiring a softer gel, allowing for easier recovery of DNA or RNA. The "others" category includes specialty agaroses designed for specific applications, such as immunology and tissue engineering, catering to diverse scientific needs.</p></p>
