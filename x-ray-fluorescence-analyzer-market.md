<p><p>An X-ray Fluorescence Analyzer (XRF) is an analytical tool used to determine the elemental composition of materials. It employs the principle of X-ray fluorescence, where a sample is irradiated with X-rays, causing the elements within to emit characteristic secondary X-rays. This technique is widely used in various industries, including metallurgy, environmental monitoring, geochemistry, and recycling due to its ability to deliver rapid, non-destructive results without extensive sample preparation.</p><p>The X-ray Fluorescence Analyzer Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by advancements in technology, increasing demand for precise material analysis, and the rise of regulatory standards in various sectors.
