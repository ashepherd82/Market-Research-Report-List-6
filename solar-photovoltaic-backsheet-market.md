<p><p>The Solar Photovoltaic Backsheet market consists of various structures designed for protective and functional purposes. KPK (Polyester-Polyester-Kraft) structures offer durability and insulation. KPE (Polyester-Polyethylene) types are known for their moisture resistance and lightweight properties. TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer) structures provide flexibility and enhanced durability. PPE (Polyphenylene Ether) structures focus on superior thermal stability and electrical insulation. Other types may include hybrid materials and innovative composites that cater to specific performance needs in solar panel applications.</p></p>
