<p><p>The auto wax market is primarily divided into two types: synthetic waxes and natural waxes. Synthetic waxes are formulated using polymers and resins, offering enhanced durability, ease of application, and superior protection against environmental factors. They often provide a high-gloss finish and water repellency. In contrast, natural waxes, derived from materials like carnauba or beeswax, are valued for their traditional appeal and eco-friendliness.
