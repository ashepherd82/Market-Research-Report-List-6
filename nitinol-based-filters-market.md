<p><strong>The Nitinol-based Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent IVC Filters</li><li>Retrievable IVC Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nitinol-based filters are primarily categorized into two market types: permanent IVC filters and retrievable IVC filters. Permanent IVC filters are designed for long-term implantation to permanently prevent embolisms in patients at risk of deep vein thrombosis. In contrast, retrievable IVC filters offer flexibility, allowing for temporary placement and subsequent removal once the risk subsides. Both types leverage the unique properties of Nitinol, a shape-memory alloy known for its biocompatibility and flexibility, enhancing their effectiveness and patient safety.</p></p>
