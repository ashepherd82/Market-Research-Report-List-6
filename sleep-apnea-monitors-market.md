<p><p>The sleep apnea monitors market encompasses several types of devices used for diagnosing and managing sleep apnea. Single channel screening devices focus on measuring basic parameters like airflow or oxygen levels, making them portable and user-friendly for initial assessments. Actigraphy systems track sleep patterns through motion sensors, providing insights into sleep quality. PSG (Polysomnography) devices are comprehensive, monitoring various physiological signals during sleep in a clinical setting, offering detailed information for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
