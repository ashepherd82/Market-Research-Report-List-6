<p><ul><li>Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI)</li><li>Port Fuel Injectors (PFI)</li><li>Diesel Fuel Injectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fuel injector market comprises three main types: Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI), Port Fuel Injectors (PFI), and Diesel Fuel Injectors. GDI systems inject fuel directly into the combustion chamber, enhancing efficiency and power. PFI systems deliver fuel into the intake manifold, providing smoother operation and lower emissions. Diesel fuel injectors, designed for compression ignition engines, offer precise fuel delivery to optimize performance and efficiency. Each type caters to specific engine technologies and performance requirements, driving innovation in the automotive sector.</p></p>
