<p><ul><li>Coil Spring</li><li>Air Spring</li><li>Leaf Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The suspension spring market includes three main types: coil springs, air springs, and leaf springs. Coil springs are widely used for their ability to absorb shocks and provide stability. Air springs utilize compressed air for customizable ride height and damping, enhancing comfort and load-carrying capacity. Leaf springs, traditionally used in heavy-duty vehicles, consist of layered metal strips that distribute loads effectively and offer durability. Each spring type caters to specific vehicle requirements, balancing performance, comfort, and load management.</p></p>
