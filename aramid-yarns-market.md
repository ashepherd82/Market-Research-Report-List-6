<p><p>The Aramid Yarns market comprises two primary types: Meta-Aramid Yarn and Para-Aramid Yarn. Meta-Aramid Yarn is known for its heat resistance, flame retardancy, and durability, making it ideal for protective clothing, filtration, and insulation. Para-Aramid Yarn offers exceptional strength, stiffness, and resistance to impact, widely used in bulletproof vests, cables, and composites. Both types are high-performance synthetic fibers, but Meta-Aramid excels in thermal stability, while Para-Aramid is superior in tensile strength, catering to diverse industrial and safety applications.</p></p>
