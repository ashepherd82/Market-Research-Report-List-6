<p><p>The Solar Control Window Films market includes three primary types: Clear (Non-Reflective), Dyed (Non-Reflective), and Vacuum Coated (Reflective). Clear films offer minimal tint while reducing UV rays and glare without altering the window's appearance. Dyed films provide increased heat rejection and privacy through added color without reflective properties. Vacuum Coated films involve a reflective metallic coating, enhancing heat and glare reduction while providing privacy. Each type serves different aesthetic preferences and functional requirements in energy efficiency and comfort.</p></p>
