<p><p>The Automatic Tag Reader market comprises primarily two types: RFID and OCR. RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) utilizes radio waves to automatically identify and track tags attached to objects, offering real-time data capture and enhanced inventory management. In contrast, OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology reads printed text from documents or labels, converting it into digital data for processing. Both technologies facilitate streamlined operations in various sectors, including logistics, retail, and manufacturing, enhancing efficiency and accuracy in data handling and inventory control.</p></p>
