<p><p>Valve Regulated Lead Acid (VRLA) batteries for vehicles primarily include Gel Cell and Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM) types. Gel Cell batteries use a silica-based gel to immobilize the electrolyte, providing enhanced safety and deep-cycle performance. AGM batteries utilize a fiberglass mat to absorb the electrolyte, offering superior discharge rates and quicker recharging. Both types are maintenance-free, resistant to leakage, and suitable for various vehicle applications, making them popular choices for automotive and recreational vehicles due to their reliability and performance.</p></p>
