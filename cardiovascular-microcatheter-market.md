<p><strong>The Cardiovascular Microcatheter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Lumen</li><li>Dual Lumen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiovascular microcatheter market comprises two primary types: single lumen and dual lumen microcatheters. Single lumen microcatheters feature one channel, allowing for straightforward navigation and delivery of therapeutic agents, making them suitable for various interventional procedures. In contrast, dual lumen microcatheters have two channels, enabling simultaneous access for infusion and guidewire passage, enhancing procedural efficiency. Both types cater to different clinical needs, influencing their applications in minimally invasive cardiovascular interventions and procedures.</p></p>
