<p><p>The Digital Baseband Unit (BBU) market application includes integrated base stations and distributed base stations. Integrated base stations combine the baseband processing unit with the radio unit in a single chassis, offering a compact and cost-effective solution. Distributed base stations, on the other hand, separate the baseband processing unit from the radio unit, allowing for greater flexibility and scalability in network deployment. Both options cater to different market needs, with integrated base stations popular in urban areas and distributed base stations preferred in rural and remote locations.</p></p>
