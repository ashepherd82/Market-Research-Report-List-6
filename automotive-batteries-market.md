<p><p>The automotive batteries market comprises several types, including VRLA (Valve-Regulated Lead-Acid) batteries, flooded batteries, and others. VRLA batteries are maintenance-free and utilize a sealed design, making them popular for modern vehicles. Flooded batteries, on the other hand, require regular maintenance and are commonly used in traditional automotive applications due to their cost-effectiveness. Other types include lithium-ion and nickel-metal hydride batteries, which are gaining traction in electric vehicles and hybrid applications, reflecting the evolving landscape of automotive technology.</p></p>
