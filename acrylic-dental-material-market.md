Powder-based acrylics are mixed with liquid monomers to create durable prosthetics like dentures. Gel forms are pre-mixed, offering convenience and ease of use for temporary restorations. Liquid acrylics are used as adhesives or for customizing dental appliances. Each type caters to different dental needs, balancing strength, workability, and application precision, making them essential in restorative and prosthetic dentistry.</p></p>
