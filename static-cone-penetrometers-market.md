<p><p>The Static Cone Penetrometer (SCP) market is primarily categorized into two types: Hand Operated Static Cone Penetrometers and Self-Start Static Cone Penetrometers. In contrast, Self-Start Static Cone Penetrometers feature automated mechanisms for driving the cone, enhancing efficiency and depth accuracy for larger-scale applications, often used in construction and geotechnical investigations.
