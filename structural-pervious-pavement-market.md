<p><p>The structural pervious pavement market comprises three main types: pervious concrete, porous asphalt, and interlocking concrete pavers. Pervious concrete features a porous structure that allows water to drain through, reducing runoff and promoting groundwater recharge. Porous asphalt utilizes a similar concept, offering a flexible surface with voids to facilitate water drainage. Interlocking concrete pavers, designed with gaps between units, also enhance water infiltration while providing durable, aesthetically pleasing surfaces. Together, these materials contribute to sustainable urban drainage solutions.</p></p>
