<p><p>The medical radiation sterilization equipment market includes three main types: E-Beam Radiation Sterilization, X-Ray Sterilization, and Gamma Sterilization. E-Beam sterilization utilizes high-energy electron beams to eliminate microorganisms quickly, making it ideal for heat-sensitive items. X-Ray sterilization employs high-energy X-rays, offering deep penetration for larger items while maintaining material integrity. Gamma sterilization uses gamma rays from radioactive isotopes, effectively sterilizing a wide range of medical products. Each method is crucial for ensuring the safety and sterility of medical devices and supplies.</p></p>
