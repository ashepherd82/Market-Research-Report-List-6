<p><ul><li>Instrument Landing System CAT I</li><li>Instrument Landing System CAT II</li><li>Instrument Landing System CAT III</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Instrument Landing System (ILS) market is categorized into three main types based on precision and operational capability. CAT I provides basic precision guidance under favorable conditions, suitable for most airports. CAT II enhances this with lower visibility operations, allowing for landings in reduced weather conditions. CAT III offers the highest level of automation, enabling landings in very low visibility situations and is further split into IIIA, IIIB, and IIIC, each with progressively stricter requirements for aircraft and airport infrastructure.</p></p>
