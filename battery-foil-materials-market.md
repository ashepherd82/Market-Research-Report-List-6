<p><p>The battery foil materials market is primarily divided into two types: cathode foil materials and anode foil materials. Cathode foil materials are used in the positive electrode of batteries, playing a crucial role in determining energy density and overall performance. In contrast, anode foil materials are utilized in the negative electrode, affecting charge storage and discharge rates. Both types of foils are essential for battery efficiency, safety, and longevity, contributing significantly to advancements in energy storage technologies.</p></p>
