<p><strong>The Heart Pump Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Implantable Heart Pump Equipment</li><li>Extracorporeal Heart Pump Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Heart Pump Equipment market can be divided into two main types: Implantable Heart Pump Equipment and Extracorporeal Heart Pump Equipment. Implantable heart pumps are devices that are surgically implanted into the body to help pump blood when the heart is unable to do so effectively. Extracorporeal heart pumps, on the other hand, are external devices that are used temporarily to assist the heart in pumping blood during surgeries or in cases of severe heart failure. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in treating heart conditions and maintaining cardiovascular health.</p></p>
