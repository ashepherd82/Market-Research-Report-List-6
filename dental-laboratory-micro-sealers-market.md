<p><p>Dental laboratory micro-sealers are essential for ensuring the durability and longevity of dental restorations. There are three main types of micro-sealers on the market: laser, infrared, and Pulse ARC. Laser micro-sealers utilize a focused beam of light to seal the restoration material, while infrared micro-sealers use heat to achieve the same effect. Pulse ARC micro-sealers, on the other hand, utilize an electrical pulse to seal the material effectively. Each type of micro-sealer offers unique advantages in terms of precision, efficiency, and effectiveness in dental laboratories.</p></p>
