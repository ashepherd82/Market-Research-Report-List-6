<p><p>The radiology stretchers market comprises three primary types: fixed stretchers, adjustable stretchers, and stretcher chairs. Fixed stretchers are designed for steady positioning and are ideal for stable patients. Adjustable stretchers allow for height and angle modifications, enhancing accessibility and comfort during procedures. Stretcher chairs combine the functionalities of a chair and a stretcher, enabling easy patient transfer and positioning in tight spaces, making them versatile for both imaging and transport in healthcare settings.</p></p>
