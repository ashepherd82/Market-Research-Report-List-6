<p><ul><li>Low Field Strength</li><li>Mid Field Strength</li><li>High Field Strength</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical MRI scanners market is categorized into three main types based on magnetic field strength: low field strength, mid field strength, and high field strength. Low field strength MRI scanners, typically below 1.0 Tesla, are cost-effective and suitable for basic imaging needs but provide lower resolution. Mid field strength scanners, ranging from 1.0 to 1.5 Tesla, balance cost and image quality, while high field strength scanners, above 1.5 Tesla, offer superior resolution and sensitivity, enabling detailed imaging for complex diagnoses.</p></p>
