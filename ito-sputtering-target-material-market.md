<p><strong>The ITO Sputtering Target Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plane Target</li><li>Rotating Target</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ITO sputtering target material market is primarily segmented into plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are flat and provide uniform film deposition, making them suitable for a wide range of applications, including displays and solar cells. Rotating targets, on the other hand, enhance efficiency and target utilization by continually exposing fresh material to the plasma, reducing issues like target overheating and improving deposition rates. Both types cater to specific industrial needs in electronics and optoelectronics manufacturing.</p></p>
