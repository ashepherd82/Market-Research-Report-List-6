ATP reagents are chemical solutions that react with ATP to produce measurable light, indicating contamination levels. Software integrates data from instruments, providing analytics and reporting tools to help organizations track hygiene compliance and improve sanitation processes. Together, these components enhance hygiene management across various industries.</p></p>
