<p><p>The underlayment market consists of various types, each serving distinct purposes. Cement Backer Units (CBU) offer moisture resistance for tiles, making them ideal for wet areas. Polyethylene provides a moisture barrier and is often used beneath laminate and hardwood flooring. Rubber underlayment excels in sound absorption and cushioning. Cork is eco-friendly and provides thermal insulation. Plywood serves as a sturdy base for flooring installation. Other materials include foam and vinyl, catering to diverse flooring needs and preferences.</p></p>
