<p><p>The LOW-E glass market consists of three main types: single, double, and triple LOW-E glass. Single LOW-E glass features a single thin coating that reflects heat, enhancing energy efficiency. Double LOW-E glass has two layers of glass with an inert gas in between, vastly improving thermal insulation and reducing energy costs. Triple LOW-E glass includes three glass panes, offering superior insulation and soundproofing, making it ideal for extreme climates. Each type caters to various building and energy efficiency needs.</p></p>
