<p><p>The synthetic silicon fertilizer market is divided into two main types: liquid and solid. Solid silicon fertilizers, on the other hand, are typically granules or powders, offering a slow-release option that nourishes the soil over time. Both forms contribute to enhancing crop resilience, boosting yields, and improving soil health, catering to diverse agricultural needs.</p></p>
