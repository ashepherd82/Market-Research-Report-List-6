<p><p>The anisotropic conductive film (ACF) market features various application types based on the substrate configurations. "Chip on Glass" refers to mounting semiconductor chips directly onto glass substrates, ideal for displays. "Chip on Flex" involves chips on flexible substrates, enhancing portability. "Chip on Board" integrates chips directly onto a board for compact designs, while "Flex on Glass" combines flexible circuits with glass for advanced displays. "Flex on Flex" enables connectivity between flexible circuits, and "Flex on Board" allows flexibility in circuit layouts for customized applications.</p></p>
