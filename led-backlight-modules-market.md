<p><p>The LED backlight modules market is segmented into three main types: Direct-Lit RGB LED, Direct-Lit White LED, and Edge-Lighted LED. Direct-Lit RGB LED utilizes red, green, and blue LEDs for vibrant colors, enhancing color accuracy and brightness. Direct-Lit White LED employs white LEDs for uniform illumination, ideal for standard displays. Edge-Lighted LED places LEDs along the edges, allowing for thinner designs and better energy efficiency, making it popular in sleek displays and screens. Each type serves specific applications and consumer needs.</p></p>
