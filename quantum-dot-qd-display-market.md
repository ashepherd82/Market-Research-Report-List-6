<p><p>Quantum Dot Display (QD) technology encompasses various types, primarily QDEF (Quantum Dot Enhancement Film) and QLED (Quantum Dot Light Emitting Diode). QDEF enhances traditional LCD displays by adding a quantum dot layer, improving color accuracy and brightness. In contrast, QLED utilizes quantum dots applied to a backlight, displaying vibrant colors with high dynamic range. Both markets are growing, driven by demands for superior visual experiences in televisions, monitors, and other devices, enhancing image quality while maintaining energy efficiency.</p></p>
