<p><strong>The TiO2 Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sulfate Process</li><li>Chloride Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TiO2 market is primarily divided into two production processes: the Sulfate Process and the Chloride Process. The Sulfate Process uses sulfuric acid to extract titanium from ilmenite or titanium slag, producing a lower-grade TiO2 suitable for industries like paints and coatings. The Chloride Process employs chlorine gas to refine high-purity titanium feedstock, yielding a superior-grade TiO2 ideal for premium applications such as plastics and cosmetics. Both processes cater to diverse market demands, balancing cost, quality, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
