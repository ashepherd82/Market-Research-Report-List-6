<p><p>Trim systems, including trim tabs and interceptors, are used to optimize vessel performance by adjusting hull trim and stability. Trim tabs are adjustable surfaces mounted on the stern, altering lift and drag to improve fuel efficiency and handling. Interceptors are vertical blades that create drag to adjust trim, offering quicker response and simpler design. Both systems cater to various vessel types, enhancing speed, stability, and comfort. The market for these systems is driven by demand for efficiency and performance in marine applications.</p></p>
