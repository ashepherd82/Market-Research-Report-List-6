<p><p>The oncology medication market comprises various types of treatments for cancer. Chemotherapy medications utilize cytotoxic agents to kill cancer cells. Targeted therapy medications focus on specific molecular targets associated with cancer, minimizing damage to normal cells. Immunotherapy medications enhance the body's immune response against tumors. Hormonal therapy medications disrupt hormone signaling that fuels certain cancers, particularly breast and prostate cancer.
