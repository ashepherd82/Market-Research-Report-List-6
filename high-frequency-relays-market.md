<p><ul><li>Surface Mount High Frequency Relays</li><li>Through Hole High Frequency Relays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Frequency Relays market is categorized into two main types: Surface Mount High Frequency Relays and Through Hole High Frequency Relays. Surface Mount High Frequency Relays are designed for compact circuit boards, allowing for efficient use of space and easier automation in manufacturing. In contrast, Through Hole High Frequency Relays feature leads that pass through the circuit board, offering enhanced structural stability and higher power handling. Both types cater to diverse applications in telecommunications, automotive, and industrial electronics, driving innovation and performance.</p></p>
