<p><ul><li>Injection Molding</li><li>Extrusion- Blow Molding</li><li>Thermoforming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-mould labeling (IML) involves integrating labels into the packaging process using various molding techniques. Injection molding creates rigid containers with labels molded directly into the surface for enhanced graphics and durability. Extrusion-blow molding combines extrusion and blow molding to produce hollow containers, allowing for unique shapes and labeling. Thermoforming forms plastic sheets into specific shapes, with labels applied during the heating process. Each method offers distinct advantages, catering to diverse packaging needs and enhancing product appeal.</p></p>
