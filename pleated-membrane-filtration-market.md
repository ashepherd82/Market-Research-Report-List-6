<p><p>The Pleated Membrane Filtration market comprises various types including PP (Polypropylene), PES (Polyethersulfone), PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene), and Nylon filters. PP filters are cost-effective and resistant to chemicals, suited for water treatment. PES filters offer high flow rates and thermal stability, ideal for pharmaceuticals. PTFE filters are hydrophobic, making them suitable for gases and aggressive solvents. Nylon filters are versatile with good wetting properties, commonly used in biological applications. Other filters may include specialty materials for niche applications, expanding the market's scope.</p></p>
