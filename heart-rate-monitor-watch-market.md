<p><strong>The Heart Rate Monitor Watch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Strapless Heart Rate Monitor</li><li>Chest Strap Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heart rate monitor watch market can be categorized into two main types: strapless heart rate monitors and chest strap monitors. Strapless monitors utilize sensors integrated into the wristwatch, providing convenience and ease of use, making them popular for casual users. In contrast, chest strap monitors offer greater accuracy by measuring heart rate directly from the chest, making them preferred by serious athletes and fitness enthusiasts. Both types serve to enhance workout efficiency and health management through real-time heart rate data.</p></p>
