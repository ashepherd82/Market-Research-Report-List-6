There are three main types of PSD markets: Full-Closed Type, which provides complete separation from the track; Semi-Closed Type, offering partial barriers for airflow while maintaining safety; and Half Height Type, which allows visibility and ventilation but still reduces accidents. Each type caters to different station designs and operational requirements, impacting installation costs and passenger experience.</p></p>
