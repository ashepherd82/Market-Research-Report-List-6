<p><p>Audio amplifiers for the marine market can be categorized into two main types: standalone and integrated. Standalone amplifiers are independent units that provide high power and sound quality, making them ideal for larger marine setups. They offer flexibility in installation and customization. In contrast, integrated amplifiers combine amplification and audio sources into a single unit, saving space and simplifying installation, which is perfect for smaller vessels.
