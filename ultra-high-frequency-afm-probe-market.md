<p><p>Arrow tip UHF AFM probes are sharp and have a narrow tip, allowing for high resolution imaging of small features on samples. Rotating tip UHF AFM probes have a tip that spins while scanning, allowing for more consistent and reliable data collection. Both types of probes are designed for use in high frequency AFM systems, which operate at frequencies higher than traditional AFM systems for faster scanning and improved resolution. These probes are used in nanotechnology, materials science, and biological research for studying surface features at the nanoscale level.</p></p>
