<p><p>The selfie stick market comprises three main types: remote-triggered, wired, and Bluetooth selfie sticks. Remote-triggered selfie sticks use a wireless remote to capture photos, allowing users to pose freely. Wired selfie sticks connect to smartphones via a jack, often providing a simple, battery-free option but limiting distance. Bluetooth selfie sticks offer wireless connectivity, allowing users to trigger the camera from a distance without physical connections, enhancing convenience and flexibility for capturing self-portraits in various settings.</p></p>
