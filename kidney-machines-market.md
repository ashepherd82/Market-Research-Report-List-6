<p><p>The kidney machines market is categorized into three main types: Continuous Blood Purification (CBP), Hemodialysis (HD), and other methods. CBP is a gradual approach to removing toxins from the blood, primarily used in critical care settings for patients with acute kidney injury. Hemodialysis involves the filtration of blood through a machine to remove waste, typically performed in outpatient settings. Other market types may include peritoneal dialysis and emerging technologies, catering to diverse patient needs and healthcare environments.</p></p>
