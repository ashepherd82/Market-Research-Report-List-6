<p><p>The RFID Safety Sensor market is categorized into contact and non-contact types. Contact RFID sensors require physical interaction with an object for data transmission, ensuring accurate readings in close-range applications. These are often used in locations where direct interactions are essential. In contrast, non-contact RFID sensors operate at a distance, allowing for automatic data capture without physical touch. This type is ideal for environments where maintaining space is crucial, enhancing efficiency in inventory management and access control systems.</p></p>
