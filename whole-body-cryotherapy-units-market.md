<p><p>Whole Body Cryotherapy (WBC) units are categorized primarily into three market types: Liquid Nitrogen Systems, Electric Systems, and Others. Liquid Nitrogen Systems utilize cryogenic gases to achieve low temperatures, facilitating quick cooling and deep tissue treatment. Electric Systems rely on electric-powered refrigeration to generate cold air, offering a more controlled and convenient experience. The "Others" category includes hybrid systems and innovative technologies that combine various cooling methods, catering to diverse user preferences and operational requirements in therapeutic and wellness settings.</p></p>
