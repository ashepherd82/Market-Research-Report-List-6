<p><p>The Pipeline Cleaning Robot market is primarily divided into two types: pressure-based cleaning robots and tool-based cleaning robots. Pressure-based cleaning robots utilize high-pressure water jets to effectively remove debris and blockages within pipelines, making them ideal for maintaining flow efficiency. In contrast, tool-based cleaning robots are equipped with various mechanical tools designed for more specialized tasks, such as cutting and scraping, to tackle tougher obstructions. Both types play a crucial role in pipeline maintenance and infrastructure management.</p></p>
