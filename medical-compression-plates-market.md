<p><p>Medical compression plates are specialized devices used in orthopedic surgeries to stabilize fractured bones and promote healing. The demand for minimally invasive surgical techniques is also contributing to market growth, as these plates facilitate quicker recovery and reduced hospital stays.</p><p>Recent trends in the market include the development of innovative materials, such as bio-resorbable plates that eliminate the need for subsequent surgical removal. Furthermore, the integration of smart technology in compression plate design is gaining traction, with the potential for real-time monitoring of healing processes.
