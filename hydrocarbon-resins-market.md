<p><ul><li>C5, Aliphatic Resins</li><li>C9, Aromatic Resins</li><li>C5/C9, Aliphatic/Aromatic Resins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydrocarbon resins are synthetic polymers derived from petroleum-based feedstocks, categorized by their chemical composition and properties. **C5 resins** are aliphatic, derived from C5 olefins, offering light color and good tackiness. **C9 resins** are aromatic, made from C9 streams, providing darker color and higher thermal stability. **C5/C9 resins** are hybrid, combining aliphatic and aromatic properties for balanced performance. Aliphatic resins are non-polar and light-colored, while aromatic resins are polar and darker. These resins are used in adhesives, coatings, and rubber compounding, tailored to specific application needs.</p></p>
