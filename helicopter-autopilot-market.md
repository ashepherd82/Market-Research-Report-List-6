<p><p>The helicopter autopilot market includes several types, each serving specific functions. Attitude Hold maintains the helicopter's orientation by automatically adjusting control inputs. Coupled systems integrate with navigation or flight management systems, allowing for automated navigation and precision control. Stability Augmentation Systems (SAS) enhance stability and control, assisting pilots in turbulent conditions. Additionally, there are other systems that provide unique functionalities, such as auto-hover or altitude hold, catering to various operational requirements within the helicopter industry.</p></p>
