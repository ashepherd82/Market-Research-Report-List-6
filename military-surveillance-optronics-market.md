<p><p>The military surveillance optronics market is categorized into multispectral and hyperspectral segments. In contrast, hyperspectral optronics analyze a wider range of wavelengths, enabling detailed material identification and more precise environmental monitoring. Both technologies are integral for modern military operations, offering improved reconnaissance, situational awareness, and threat assessment through advanced imaging and sensor capabilities.</p></p>
