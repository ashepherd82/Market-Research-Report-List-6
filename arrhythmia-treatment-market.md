<p><strong>The Arrhythmia Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The arrhythmia treatment market encompasses various methodologies. Medication involves antiarrhythmic drugs that stabilize heart rhythm. Cardioversion utilizes electrical shocks to restore normal rhythm. Catheter ablation targets and destroys abnormal heart tissue causing irregular beats. Pacemakers are implanted devices that help regulate heartbeats, while implantable cardioverter-defibrillators (ICDs) detect and correct life-threatening arrhythmias. Each treatment option addresses specific types of arrhythmias, varying in invasiveness and suitability depending on patient condition and arrhythmia type, contributing to a comprehensive approach to management.</p></p>
