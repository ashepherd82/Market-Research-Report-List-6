<p><ul><li>Vapor Compression Systems</li><li>Cryogenic Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chilled products transport encompasses two primary market types: vapor compression systems and cryogenic systems. Vapor compression systems use refrigerants to remove heat from products, maintaining low temperatures during transportation. These systems are widely used due to their efficiency and reliability. Cryogenic systems, on the other hand, utilize liquid gases like nitrogen to achieve extremely low temperatures, ideal for transporting sensitive materials such as pharmaceuticals and certain food products. Both systems ensure the integrity and quality of temperature-sensitive goods throughout their journey.</p></p>
