<p><p>The mobile phone camera optical filter market comprises several categories based on their functions. Bandpass filters only allow a specific range of wavelengths, enhancing colors and reducing reflections. Longpass filters transmit longer wavelengths while blocking shorter ones, improving low-light performance and contrast. Shortpass filters do the opposite, permitting shorter wavelengths and rejecting longer ones, useful for enhancing blue and green hues. Other filters encompass a variety of specialized options, catering to diverse photographic needs and enhancing image quality across different lighting conditions.</p></p>
