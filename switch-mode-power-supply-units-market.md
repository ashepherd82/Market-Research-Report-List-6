<p><p>The Switch-mode Power Supply Units (SMPS) market is primarily divided into two categories: AC/DC and DC/DC converters. AC/DC converters transform alternating current from mains electricity into direct current, making them essential for powering devices like computers and televisions. On the other hand, DC/DC converters regulate and convert direct current from one voltage level to another, often used in battery-operated devices and renewable energy applications. Both types enhance efficiency and miniaturization, catering to diverse consumer electronics and industrial needs.</p></p>
