<p><p>5G technology encompasses three key service market types: eMBB (enhanced Mobile Broadband), URLLC (Ultra-Reliable Low Latency Communications), and mMTC (massive Machine Type Communications). eMBB focuses on delivering high-speed data and improved user experiences for applications like video streaming and virtual reality. URLLC targets mission-critical applications requiring minimal latency and high reliability, such as autonomous driving and remote surgery. mMTC supports vast numbers of connected devices, ideal for IoT applications, enabling efficient data transmission among numerous sensors and smart devices.</p></p>
