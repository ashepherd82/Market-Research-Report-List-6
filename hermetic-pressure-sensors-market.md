<p><p>Hermetic pressure sensors are categorized into three market types based on their operating pressure ranges: low pressure, mid pressure, and high pressure. Low-pressure sensors typically measure gases or liquids at pressures below 10 psi, suitable for applications like HVAC and environmental monitoring. Mid-pressure sensors operate between 10 psi and 500 psi, commonly used in industrial processes. High-pressure sensors handle pressures above 500 psi, essential for oil and gas, aerospace, and automotive applications. Each type is designed for specific environmental and operating conditions.</p></p>
