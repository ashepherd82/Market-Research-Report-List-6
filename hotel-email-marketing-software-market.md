<p><p>Hotel email marketing software can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. On-premises software, however, is installed directly on the hotel's own servers, providing greater control over data and customization options, but often requires higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Each type serves different operational needs and preferences.</p></p>
