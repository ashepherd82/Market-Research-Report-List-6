<p><strong>The Soil Moisture Probes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitance Type</li><li>Resistor Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soil moisture probes market comprises mainly two types: capacitance-type and resistive-type sensors. Capacitance-type probes measure soil moisture by assessing the dielectric constant, providing quick and accurate readings without significant soil disturbance. In contrast, resistive-type probes measure soil moisture by gauging the electrical resistance between two electrodes, which can be less accurate and more affected by soil composition. Each type has its applications, with capacitance sensors widely favored for agriculture and environmental monitoring due to their reliability and durability.</p></p>
