<p><p>The Flying Probe Testers market consists of two main types: Single Sided Probing Testers and Dual Sided Probing Testers. Single Sided Probing Testers are designed to test one side of a printed circuit board (PCB) at a time, providing efficient testing for simple designs. In contrast, Dual Sided Probing Testers can simultaneously access both sides of a PCB, enabling more complex testing and higher throughput. Each type serves varying needs based on PCB complexity and production volume, catering to diverse electronic manufacturing requirements.</p></p>
