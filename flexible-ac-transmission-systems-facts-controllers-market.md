<p><strong>The Flexible AC Transmission Systems (FACTS) Controllers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Series Controllers</li><li>Shunt Controllers</li><li>Shunt-Series Controllers</li><li>Series-Series Controllers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexible AC Transmission Systems (FACTS) controllers enhance the reliability and efficiency of power transmission networks. Series controllers, like thyristor-controlled series capacitors, improve voltage stability by injecting capacitance into lines. Shunt controllers, such as static VAR compensators, manage reactive power to regulate voltage levels. Shunt-series controllers combine both functionalities, allowing for dynamic voltage and reactive power management. Series-series controllers, featuring interconnected series devices, optimize power flow across multiple transmission lines, ensuring balanced load distribution and enhanced system performance.</p></p>
