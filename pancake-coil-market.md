<p><p>Pancake coils are vital components in various market segments like ignition coils and inductance coils. Ignition coils transform battery voltage into the high voltage required to ignite the air-fuel mixture in internal combustion engines, ensuring efficient engine performance. Inductance coils, on the other hand, store energy and release it in electrical circuits, playing a crucial role in transformers and electrical applications. Both types leverage the unique structure of pancake coils for improved efficiency, compactness, and enhanced electromagnetic performance.</p></p>
