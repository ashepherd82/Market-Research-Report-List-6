<p><p>The oil and gas market utilizes two main types of pumps: centrifugal and positive displacement pumps. Centrifugal pumps are designed to move fluids through rotational energy, making them ideal for high flow rates and low viscosity liquids. In contrast, positive displacement pumps operate by trapping a fixed volume of fluid and forcing it through the pump, making them suitable for high-viscosity materials and applications requiring precise flow control. Both pump types are essential for efficient fluid management in the industry.</p></p>
