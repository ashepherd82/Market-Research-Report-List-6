<p><p>The biological insecticide market comprises three main types: microbial pesticides, plant pesticides, and biochemical pesticides. Microbial pesticides utilize natural microorganisms like bacteria and fungi to target pests, offering a specific and environmentally friendly solution. Plant pesticides are derived from plant extracts, harnessing compounds that repel or inhibit insect feeding. Biochemical pesticides, on the other hand, consist of naturally occurring chemicals that disrupt pest behavior or development, providing a sustainable alternative to synthetic chemicals in pest management strategies.</p></p>
