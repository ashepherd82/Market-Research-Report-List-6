<p><p>A mechanical pipette controller is a handheld device used in laboratories to accurately and efficiently aspirate and dispense liquids. Mechanical pipette controllers are preferred for their simplicity, durability, and cost-effectiveness compared to electronic alternatives.</p><p>The Mechanical Pipette Controller Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.4% during the forecast period.
